About This Assessment

The S-Strengths Assessment is a comprehensive personality and strengths evaluation tool designed to help you discover your unique combination of talents across 34 distinct themes.

This assessment is inspired by the CliftonStrengths methodology but is an independent implementation and does not represent the official CliftonStrengths assessment developed by Gallup, Inc.

What You'll Discover

  • Your Top 5 Strengths: Identify your dominant talents
  • Complete Theme Ranking: See all 34 themes ranked by your responses
  • Four Domains: Understand your strengths across Executing, Influencing, Relationship Building, and Strategic Thinking
  • Detailed Insights: Learn what you do well and potential challenges for each strength

Assessment Details

  • 40 questions covering all 34 strength themes
  • Takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete
  • Export your results to PDF or CSV
  • No registration required
